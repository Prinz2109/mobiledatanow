Note: You can skip this step if you want to just start using the application. This is related to managing your installation, your account details and looking at purchase receipts. 

 

When you activated MobileDataNow with your email address, you will have been sent an email which allows you to login at www.mobiledatanow.com/user to manage your installations, change your details and look at past purchases.

 

Click the login link in the email, change your password and click the Submit button

 

Next click the My Installations icon to the left.

 

 

 

You should change the name from Free Installation to something more meaningful, especially if you plan to install MobileDataNow to many different computers. This will make it easier to keep track of purchases for each computer.

 

To change the installation name, click Edit and change the Installation Name.
