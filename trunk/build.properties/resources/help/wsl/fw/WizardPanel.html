Wizard Screens

A wizard is designed to simplify the task of entering several screens of related data.

A wizard screen contains several wizard panels that are displayed one at a time in sequence. You navigate between the wizard panels by pressing the Next and Prev (previous) buttons.

When all the required screens have been traversed the required data has been entered pressing the Finish button will save the data and close the wizard.

At any time pressin the Cancel button will close the wizard without saving the changes.
